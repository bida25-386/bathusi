About Us

Discover the story behind Gaborone's premier fitness destination.

Our Story

Fitzone gym was incorporated on the 21 of January 2025. Located in Gaborone near Game City mall along A1 Lobatse road, we established this gym as a modern facility to serve the growing community.

Since our construction, we have continuously expanded, quickly becoming the most well-known and highly regarded fitness facility in the area. We are dedicated to promoting a healthier lifestyle for all our members.

Our Mission

To inspire people to achieve their fitness goals through professional training and a supportive environment.

Our Vision

To become the leading fitness and wellness center in Botswana.

Our Purpose & Promise

Healthy Living

To promote physical lifestyle and healthy living across the community.

Stress Relief

To provide recreational and stress relief activities through energetic workouts.

Professional Guidance

To offer professional fitness guidance and training from certified experts.

Athletic Endurance

To enhance athletic endurance for people who take part in sports competitions.
